Handler for the instruction TON.

Produces a speaker output representing the tone specified in the instruction's data.

Warning
The tone must be provided in the following format: C#7, where C is any tone (C, D, E, F, G, A, H), 7 is the pitch (choose from 1-7) and # indicates the semitone higher then C. # is optional.
